Lasagna

Description

Want to rock Italian food but don't know where to start? Here we have gathered the best recipe to make the perfect lasagna!

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the riccota mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
