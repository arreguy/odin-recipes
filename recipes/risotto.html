Risotto

Description

A very simple recipe for portobello and white mushroom risotto with plenty of flavor. Dare to try it!

Ingredients

  • Chicken broth
  • Olive oil
  • Mushrooms
  • Shallots
  • Arborio rice
  • White wine
  • Butter
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Chives

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Warm broth in a saucepan over low heat.
  3. Add portobello and white mushrooms; cook and stir until soft, about 3 minutes.
  4. Pour in wine and warm broth to the rice, and stir until it's been absorbed.
  5. Remove from heat. Stir in reserved mushrooms and their liquid, butter, chives, and Parmesan cheese.
  6. Season with salt and pepper and serve immediately!
