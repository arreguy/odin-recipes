Pizza

Description

Let's learn how to make pizza dough? This pizza dough recipe is delicious, fluffy and will go perfect with all the toppings you can imagine!

Ingredients

  • Yeast
  • Sugar
  • Bread flour
  • Olive oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Mix the ingredients.
  2. Let the dough come to room temperature.
  3. Prepare your surface with oil.
  4. Shape the dough.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown, 15 to 20 minutes.
